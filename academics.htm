Making the Grade

Delta Tau Delta believes that in order for its members to succeed as individuals, the fraternity must provide an environment in which learning and growth are encouraged as a vital part of everyday life. 

Delt Tools for Academic Success

As a member of our chapter, there are several key resources available to you that will help you navigate academic life at the University of Arizona.

Living In
At our newly remodeled house -- located right on Greek Row -- you'll find that Sundays through Thursdays we maintain a dedicated "study room " that is free from noise and distraction, making the Delt house the ideal place to study.

Director of Academic Affairs
Our Director of Academic Affairs is in charge of our Academic Program. He is responsible for making sure that every brother performs well in their schoolwork.  If a brother falls behind academically, the Director of Academic Affairs will work with the member to ensure academic success.

Real Ambition
At Delta Tau Delta, academics are our first priority. Let's face it: you're in college to get an education, and we view academic excellence as a key component in any life that attempts to exude overall excellence.

Peer Mentors
Essential to our academic success is the Delt "Big Brother" tradition, where an older Delt member becomes a new initiates mentor. Oftentimes the Big Brother has already taken many of the same classes and turns out to be a huge asset to the new initiates classes.

Past Performance
Delta Tau Delta consistently ranks among the top of all fraternities with regards to academics. For the last 5 years, Delta Tau Delta has found itself as a leading fraternity in terms of GPA. Nationally, our current GPA of 2.95 places us in the top 5% of all national fraternities.
